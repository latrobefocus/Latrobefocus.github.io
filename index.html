Welcome to La Trobe FOCUS

Focus Fellowship Of Overseas Christian University Students-

Be part of something bigger.

Meet Jesus Make Friends

FOCUS aims to help International students at La Trobe University make new friends and find out about Jesus and why he is worth having a friendship with. Each Friday we have an international meal together, sing some songs, study the bible, attend workshops and pray together. You don't have to be a Christian to come along. We welcome anyone who is interested. So, if you are interested in Christian fellowship, learning about God and Jesus, going to social events and making new friends, then FOCUS is for you.

Listen to what our people have to say about FOCUS

The Time is always Right to do what is Right

FOCUS is, to me, a place where I grow to know more about who God is. FOCUS has been a great community where I have made my closest friends at uni who are always encouraging and supporting me.

Calista Ng

If anyone were to ask me what the highlight of my university life, I would say.. FOCUS! I came to Australia from Malaysia as a first year Health Sciences student. I’ve had bad experiences in clubs and gave up on the idea of joining any. After much persuasion from a friend, I had decided to give FOCUS a try. Honestly, I was happily surprised! I met a good bunch of people who not only remained friends with me but became family – all the way to my fourth year of uni! My FOCUS family has helped me through my most difficult time in life and also shared the biggest joys, too. I have also rediscovered the gospel – who Jesus is and what He did on the cross for me, for us, for the world. I would personally recommend FOCUS to any student looking for a community to belong to and asking big questions about life!

Liesl Tan

Living in a foreign country can be a very daunting thing. It is totally out of my comfort zone. And it is always hard that my community is far away from where I am. FOCUS is a really good place for me to make friends and build my social circle where I can always get help from. Nevertheless, it is a place where I can grow deeper in my knowledge of God’s words.

Eunice Ko
