Welcome to La Trobe FOCUS

Focus Fellowship Of Overseas Christian University Students-

Be part of Something Bigger.

Meet Jesus Make Friends

FOCUS aims to help International students at La Trobe University make new friends and find out about Jesus and why he is worth having a friendship with. Each Friday we have an international meal together, sing some songs, study the bible, attend workshops and pray together. You don't have to be a Christian to come along. We welcome anyone who is interested. So, if you are interested in Christian fellowship, learning about God and Jesus, going to social events and making new friends, then FOCUS is for you.
